Restore Hope, Save Cows

In many parts of rural India, cows are symbols of compassion, peace, and life. Yet too many face neglect, malnutrition, and disease. At GauPrem (CowLove), our mission is simple: rescue suffering cows and provide the food, medical care, and safe shelter they desperately need.

Our mission

Rescue

Identify cows suffering from hunger, disease, or abandonment and bring them to safety.

Care

Provide nutritious fodder, clean water, and veterinary care including vaccines and medicines.

Shelter

Offer a clean, secure space to recover, with compassionate daily supervision.

FAQ

Are donations really used only for cows?

Yes. Every penny is guaranteed to be used to feed impoverished cows in India and provide necessary medical care and shelter. We share receipts and updates.

Is GauPrem a nonprofit?

GauPrem is a volunteer-run nonprofit initiative focused on rescue and care. We operate transparently and dedicate funds to direct essentials.

How can I get updates?

Donate on GoFundMe and opt into updates, or email us to receive monthly summaries with photos and expense breakdowns.
